Dr. Roxane L. Gervais
Roxane is an Occupational Psychologist and Organisational Psychologist specialising in work- related stress, occupational safety and health (OSH) and well-being. She is chartered by and registered with the British Psychological Society, and regulated by the Health and Care Professions Council.

Roxane is an Associate Fellow of the British Psychological Society and is registered as a Chartered Scientist with the British Psychological Society. Roxane has worked in the Americas, across Europe and in the Caribbean. She has over thirty years of experience in supporting organisations.

Roxane’s expertise and experience in addition to the areas listed above incorporates work engagement, behaviour change, leadership development, supporting senior leaders at all levels in the organisation, supporting diversity, effectiveness of communication techniques, the work-life interface, organisational culture, organisational climate, organisational change, and assessing the aptitude, abilities and personality of workers. Roxane regularly speaks and is invited to speak at national and international conferences.
